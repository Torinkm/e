Health Page

Here are some tips for staying healthy!
(Hover over any tips for an explanation!)








You Should get plenty of sleep everyday!

Consider sleep as a key to your recovery which helps rebuilds your muscles when you workout and makes you much stronger in the long run


You should maintain proper form when doing physical exercises!

When working out you'll be strength training, you'll be putting your body through very strenuous activity, so its important to maintain proper form


You should control your portion sizes

By keeping your portions in check you will end up losing weight by choosing a healthy amount of certain foods










You should start small when exercising!

Even if your fitness goals are to be able to run long disctances or lift heavy weights, it's very important that you build up to it gradually


You should only do what you enjoy doing!

If going to the gym, running, etc isn't for you, make sure to consider what you think you will enjoy the most as it will make exercise seem like less of a chore and something you will look forward to


Remember doing something is better than doing nothing

Some days can turn out to be more busier than others. It is important to note that even a small amount of exercise is better than nothing at all
